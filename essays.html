Why you should learn coding?

An epiphany stuck me today which is "everyone should learn to code" and here is why. So, first of all we know that to learn a concept yourself it is better to teach someone as it helps in understanding the concept better and finding out the gaps in knowledge. But you cant always have a person to teach you at your availability, but you have that one thing always lying on your desk which understands the concepts you explain to it but in a very specific form which we call the programming language. I would say with proper set of instructions supplied to it the computers are the dumbest thing even dumber than an animals.
